HIV Transmission Risk Meta-Analysis

Comprehensive systematic review analyzing 101 studies with 1.3M participants to identify transmission routes associated with advanced HIV disease. Implemented Bayesian network meta-analysis for precise risk stratification across MSM, IDU, and heterosexual contact groups.

Key Findings:

  • Heterosexual contact showed highest risk for advanced HIV disease
  • Network meta-analysis revealed significant odds ratios: IDU (OR=0.56) and MSM (OR=0.54) vs HC
  • Regions with criminalized sex work had 30% higher AHD risk
  • Pooled AHD prevalence: 34.87% globally
