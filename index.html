Atherosclerosis Endotype Discovery

Applied neural networks and hierarchical clustering to identify 4 distinct carotid atherosclerosis endotypes from 3,340 participants. Novel approach combining clinical data with advanced ML algorithms for personalized cardiovascular risk assessment.

Key Findings:

  • Identified 4 distinct atherosclerosis endotypes using unsupervised learning

Epidemiology • Network Meta-Analysis

HIV Transmission Risk Meta-Analysis

Comprehensive systematic review analyzing 101 studies with 1.3M participants to identify transmission routes associated with advanced HIV disease. Implemented Bayesian network meta-analysis for precise risk stratification across MSM, IDU, and heterosexual contact groups.

Key Findings:

  • Heterosexual contact showed highest risk for advanced HIV disease
  • Network meta-analysis revealed significant odds ratios: IDU (OR=0.56) and MSM (OR=0.54) vs HC
  • Regions with criminalized sex work had 30% higher AHD risk
  • Pooled AHD prevalence: 34.87% globally

Clinical Data • LASSO Regression

COVID-19 Mortality Prediction Model

Developed ANDC early warning score using LASSO regression analysis of 301 COVID-19 patients. Four-parameter model (Age, Neutrophil-to-lymphocyte ratio, D-dimer, CRP) achieved superior predictive performance for mortality risk.

Key Findings:

  • ANDC score: Age, NLR, D-dimer, CRP parameters
  • Superior AUC performance vs individual biomarkers
  • Early risk stratification for clinical decision-making
  • Validated across multiple patient cohorts

Genomics • Large-Scale Analysis

Atherosclerosis GWAS Discovery

Large-scale genome-wide association study analyzing 24,811 participants to identify genetic determinants of coronary atherosclerosis. Discovered 20 significant genetic markers associated with coronary plaque burden using advanced statistical genomics.

Key Findings:

  • 20 genome-wide significant loci for coronary plaque burden
  • Novel genetic architecture of subclinical atherosclerosis
  • Post GWAS analysis integration including eQTLs, genetic correlation & colocalization
  • Mendelian randomization and polygenic risk score development
